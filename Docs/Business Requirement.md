# Blinkit Performance Analysis | Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and outlet distribution to uncover actionable insights that support revenue optimization, product strategy, and operational efficiency.

The analysis is designed to enable stakeholders to:

- Evaluate overall business performance
- Understand product-level and outlet-level dynamics
- Identify key drivers of sales and customer satisfaction
- Support data-driven decision-making

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following metrics will be used to evaluate business performance:

- **Total Sales:** Total revenue generated across all items
- **Average Sales:** Average revenue per item
- **Number of Items Sold:** Total volume of items sold
- **Average Rating:** Mean customer rating across all items

These KPIs form the foundation for all subsequent analysis.

## Analytical Requirements

The analysis will deliver multi-dimensional insights across product attributes, outlet characteristics, and location-based performance.

### Product-Level Analysis

#### Sales by Fat Content

**Objective:**  
Evaluate the impact of product fat content on sales performance and customer satisfaction.

**Scope:**

- Total sales by fat category
- Comparative evaluation using defined KPIs

#### Sales by Item Type

**Objective:**  
Assess the contribution of product categories to overall revenue and performance.

**Scope:**

- Total sales by item type
- KPI-based comparison across categories

### Outlet Performance Analysis

#### Sales by Outlet (Fat Content Segmentation)

**Objective:**  
Analyze how product composition performs across different outlet types and locations.

**Scope:**

- Total sales by outlet segmented by fat content
- KPI comparison across outlet segments

#### Sales by Outlet Establishment

**Objective:**  
Evaluate how outlet characteristics such as establishment type or age influence sales performance.

**Scope:**

- Total sales by outlet establishment attributes
- Comparative performance analysis across outlet groups

#### Sales Contribution by Outlet Size

**Objective:**  
Determine the relationship between outlet size and revenue generation.

**Scope:**

- Percentage contribution to total sales by outlet size
- Identification of high-performing outlet segments

#### Sales by Outlet Location

**Objective:**  
Analyze geographic distribution of sales to identify high-performing location tiers.

**Scope:**

- Total sales by outlet location tier
- Comparative regional performance analysis

### Performance by Outlet Type

**Objective:**  
Provide a consolidated view of performance across different outlet formats.

**Scope:**

- KPI-based comparison across outlet types

## Expected Outcomes

- Clear visibility into key revenue drivers
- Identification of high-performing products and outlet segments
- Insights into customer preferences and satisfaction trends
- Improved understanding of operational performance across outlets

## Deliverables

- Structured SQL queries for KPI computation and analysis
- Clean, analysis-ready dataset
- Documented analytical logic and business rules
